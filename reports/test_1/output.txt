Ah, caprese pasta! It's a simple but delightful dish. Here's how you can make it:

Ingredients:
- 14 oz (400g) pasta of your choice (like spaghetti or penne)
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- 8 oz (225g) fresh mozzarella cheese, diced
- 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and minced garlic.
3. Add the cooked pasta to the bowl and toss everything together gently.
4. Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil over the pasta and toss again to combine.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
6. Serve warm or at room temperature. Buon appetito!
